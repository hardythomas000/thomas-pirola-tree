## Notes

Married twice — both Langley sisters:
1. **Jessica Cornelia Langley** (died) → daughter Jessie
2. **Frances Elizabeth Langley** (Fanny) → Florence May, Violet, **Grace**, Arthur Charles (killed WWI), Stanley (killed WWI)

Both Arthur and Fanny died fairly young. Six children dispersed to different relatives.
