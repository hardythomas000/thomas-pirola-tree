## Notes

Born September 1936, **Mexico City** (birth cert: 'Year of the Reform 359'). Conceived in Chile. Scraped into Sydney University at 15.

Met Mavis at uni party. Married shortly after graduating. Perth (4 years) → Sydney → London → New York (3 years) → Sydney.

Co-founded Pancreatic Research Group UNSW 1981. 100+ publications. OAM (1997), Knight of St Gregory (1998), Honorary Doctorate ACU (2016). Pontifical Council for the Family (27 years, 3 Popes).

At 86 (2023): 'I'm only doing one thing and that is pancreatic research.'
