## Notes

Middle name "MacAlister" is a Scottish clan name, consistent with the Gibson family's Kilmaurs, Scotland origins. Mother's full name Elizabeth May Jones (previously known only as "May Jones" from the Gibson family tree document).

Gibson family were Scottish sugar pioneers in Queensland. Descended from [[William Gibson Snr]] (b. 1813, Kilmaurs, Scotland) who established Clydesdale Mill and Bingera Plantation.
