## Notes

6th of 17 children. Also a cheesemaker. Married Maria Rinaldi. ~1930: moved to Milan, ran highly successful grocery business. Mild-mannered, absent-minded — took tram in slippers. Built family mausoleum. Five of his sisters became nuns (one, Matilda, in enclosed Jerusalem convent).
