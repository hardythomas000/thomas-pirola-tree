## Notes

Youngest. Boarding school **St Ignatius** during Grace's illness. Outstanding footballer. Won rowing regatta — first in school history.

**Fordham University**, New York — football, almost qualified for US Olympic fours rowing team. Met and married MaryJo.

Settled Australia. 7 children (one, Joey, died in infancy — congenital heart). **Marketing manager of BHP**. Gave up BHP for quieter position to focus on faith. Lived in Manila then Melbourne.

**Died age 51, heart attack at gym after work.** Buried Cheltenham, VIC.
