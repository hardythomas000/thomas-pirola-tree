## Notes

'Looked like Elizabeth Taylor.' Bright and vivacious, spoilt by father. Married son of shipping magnate in New York, 2 sons. Divorced then widowed.

Age ~29: developed **multiple sclerosis**. Prayer healing service → disease became static. Moved to Arizona (near Tombstone) for dry climate. Married American war veteran (paraplegia → killed when hit by car in wheelchair). Two days later, Gaie had heart arrhythmia → permanent brain damage.

Now in aged care home in Phoenix, Arizona. Byron Pirola is her guardian.
