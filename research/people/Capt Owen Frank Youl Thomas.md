## Notes

One of Qantas's earliest pilots. WWII: civilian pilot ferrying people to/from occupied Singapore in a float plane. Commanded Avro Lancastrian G-AGLX — disappeared over Indian Ocean 23 Mar 1946. 10 on board, never found.
