## Notes

Born 1933, **Barcelona, Spain**. Anglicized to 'George' on arrival in Australia. Short, red-headed, rebellious. 'The guy with all the pizzaz' — danced on restaurant tables. Famous for riding red motor scooter through peak-hour traffic on Sydney Harbour Bridge.

Worked at **E.P.T.** (Electric Power Transmission) Melbourne → **Transfield** North Sydney. Both Italian construction companies that built power lines for the **Snowy Scheme**. Spoke some Italian, innovative despite no engineering training.

Married [[Roma Spalding]] in Melbourne, December 1958. Parents of four children.

**Died suddenly at age 49.**
