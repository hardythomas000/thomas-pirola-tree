## Notes

Gino Sr's brother. Took over father's grocery business in Milan. Married **Lina** from Calabria (scandal — 'for a Milanese to marry a Southerner was a major disgrace'). WWII: fought in Russia, walked back across Europe in winter alone. Children: Bruno (Brazil), Lydia (married Gianfelice).
