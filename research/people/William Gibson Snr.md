## Notes

Born **Kilmaurs, Scotland**. Married Mary McAllister at Kilmarnock 1838. 4 sons, 3 daughters.

Arrived Brisbane July 1863 with eldest son Angus on *Cairngorm*. Sugar pioneers: **Clydesdale Mill** at Hemmant 1868 → **Bingera Plantation** 1883 (1,318 acres, Bundaberg). Used **Kanaka labour** — noted for providing some of the worst conditions.

Died age 89 at Bingera, December 1902.
