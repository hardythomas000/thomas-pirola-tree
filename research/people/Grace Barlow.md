## Notes

English. Great-grandfather [[Frederick Barlow]] was sea captain from Nottingham. Grandfather [[Arthur Charles Radcliffe Barlow]] married twice — both Langley sisters.

As teenager, worked in London doctor's home. Fell on stairs, injured back → sent to **Prof Auguste Rollier's sanatorium at Leysin**, Switzerland. Studied at **Louvain University** (Belgium). Child-minding in France where she met Gino.

Spoke 5 languages: Italian, French, German, Spanish, English. Artistic: oil painting, poetry. Loved opera and Strauss waltzes. High Church of England → Catholic convert.

Developed mental health issues from ~1940s (paranoia, possible neurological condition). Improved in 60s. Died peacefully in her sleep, age 62. Buried Macquarie Park Cemetery.
