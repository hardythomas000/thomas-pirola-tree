## Notes

Birth name: **Roma Spalding**. Subud name: Haryanti. Later married Mr. Stuart.

Married [[Gino Pirola Jr]] in Melbourne, December 1958. Grace (Gino's mother) 'always understood him better and took his side... a very caring person.'

Parents of four children (including [[Hanna Thomas]]). Long-time Subud Voice subscriber in Australia.
