## Notes

Born Sondrio/Cernusco area. At 12, left school during WWI, delivery boy. Army: laid telephone lines in Alps. Accomplished boxer. Office job at **Chatillon** (synthetic textiles) → **Italviscosa** (3rd largest in world after Courtalds & DuPont). Nickname at work: **'the lion'**. Honoured with **Stella di Lavoro** by Italian Government.

Met [[Grace Barlow]] on business trip to France — communicated in French.

**Life:** Barcelona 1932 → Mexico City 1936 → Milan 1939 → Sydney 1939 → Shanghai post-war → escaped 1949 (last plane out) → settled Australia 1950. Naturalized 30 Jun 1955.

After Grace's death, remarried **Patricia Hildebrandt** (25 years). Died age ~97 of heart attack at transport department renewing driver's licence. Philosophy courses at Sydney University in his early 90s.
