# Open Questions

Research gaps and unresolved questions, organized by priority and family line.

## High Priority (Would Significantly Advance Understanding)

### 1. Who are Hardy's parents?
- **The gap**: No names, dates, or locations recorded yet
- **Impact**: Unlocks entire paternal and maternal research lines
- **Next step**: Ask Hardy for full names, birth dates, locations
- **Solvability**: HIGH (Hardy knows this)
- **Payoff**: HIGH

### 2. What is the origin of the Thomas surname in this family?
- **The gap**: Thomas could be English, Welsh, Cornish, or adopted/anglicized
- **Impact**: Determines which geographic archives to prioritize
- **Next step**: Trace paternal grandfather's origins
- **Solvability**: HIGH (once grandparent names known)
- **Payoff**: HIGH

### 3. Who are Hardy's wife's parents and grandparents?
- **The gap**: Indonesian family line completely undocumented
- **Impact**: Opens entire Indonesian heritage research
- **Next step**: Ask Hardy/wife for family names, locations in Indonesia
- **Solvability**: MODERATE (may depend on oral history and Indonesian records)
- **Payoff**: HIGH

### 4. What is the geographic origin within Indonesia?
- **The gap**: Children's names suggest Javanese/Malay heritage but not confirmed
- **Impact**: Determines which regional records to search
- **Next step**: Ask about family's region (Java, Sulawesi, Sumatra, etc.)
- **Solvability**: HIGH (family knows this)
- **Payoff**: MODERATE

## Medium Priority

### 5. When did the Thomas family arrive in Australia?
- **The question**: Multi-generational Australian or immigrant family?
- **Minimum records needed**: Immigration records or earliest Australian birth/marriage
- **Search strategy**: FamilySearch immigration collections, Trove shipping lists
- **Solvability**: MODERATE
- **Payoff**: MODERATE

### 6. Did Hardy's family have any connection to Melbourne before his time there?
- **The question**: Hardy worked in Melbourne 2010-2017 — was this a return or first time?
- **Minimum records needed**: Family addresses/records in VIC
- **Search strategy**: VIC BDM, electoral rolls
- **Solvability**: MODERATE
- **Payoff**: LOW
